I recently completed my Master's degree in ECE specializing in Machine Learning at University of Toronto advised by Prof. Deepa Kundur and by Prof. Yuri Lawryshyn . My research focuses on Deep Learning based Recommendation System. Apart from my research, I am also interested in Deep Learning and Deep Reinforcement Learning with the focus of transfer learning, imitation learning, model based RL and Inverse Reinforcement Learning. My ultimate goal is to build agents with human like ability to generalize in real world environments by using data as its own supervision. I am also very interested in RL based CyberSecurity.

I enjoy working with people and discussing ideas, so please do not hesitate to contact me.
